Leak Detection Services

A hidden leak can cause major problems if left untreated. At ProperPools, our expert leak detection services use advanced equipment to pinpoint leaks quickly and non-invasively.

Our Process

  • Visual and auditory inspection
  • Pressure testing and dye tracing
  • Advanced sonar/mic technology for non-destructive locating
  • Detailed report and repair recommendations

Service Notes

Pricing for leak detection is quoted individually based on the type of system and access conditions. Prompt scheduling and rapid diagnosis ensure you avoid escalating damage or water loss.
